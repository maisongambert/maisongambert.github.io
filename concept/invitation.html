WHERE IT ALL
BEGINS...

A beautiful postcard and an enchanting landscape are both attractive but don’t say much. One must sniff the perfumes, feel the textures, listen to the voices, and feel on one’s skin the heat of the sun and the salt carried by the sea wind. For this reason, it is helpful to have a guide so to not miss out on anything. Keeping in mind that tomorrow everything may be different, because we will be different, and everything can be discovered once again.

“…because land and tradition are our roots.”

invitation

Artichokes, aubergines, tomatoes and all the other vegetables that our grandmothers used to keep preserved in oil so we could feel the summer flavours in winter too, to taste on a piece of bread this treasure and knowledge. Retrieving this knowledge and passing it down is not just tradition. It is saying thank you to those who nourished us with love and give this small piece of our history as a gift to those who come and visit us. So straight from the beginning it is our presentation: this is who we are and where we come from.
